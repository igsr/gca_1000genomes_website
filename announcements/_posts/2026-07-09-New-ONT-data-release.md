---
title: "New ONT direct RNA-seq data release"
pinned: false
---

Long-read direct RNA-seq data generated using ONT are now available for 60 lymphoblastoid cell lines from the 1000 Genomes/Geuvadis project.

Raw sequencing data are available through ENA under accession PRJEB76585, with BAM files, transcript quantifications, m6A modification calls, summary statistics and analysis code also available.

More information and links to the data are available on the [ONT direct RNA-seq data collection page](/data-portal/data-collection/drs_lcls_ont_60_eur).
